About KSR College

Empowering technological excellence since 1994

K.S. Rangasamy College of Technology (KSRCT), established in 1994 near Tiruchengode, Tamil Nadu, is a premier autonomous engineering institution affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE. Spread across a scenic campus, KSRCT offers 14 undergraduate, 11 postgraduate, and 20 Ph.D. programmes :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

The college holds autonomous status from UGC and is accredited by NBA (Tier‑1) and NAAC with an A++ grade. It ranks among the top institutions under NIRF’s engineering category and is recognized by QS. KSRCT also boasts sponsorship from DST‑FIST and strong research capabilities with multi‑crore grants in technical textiles and geotextiles, and active R&D collaborations with ISRO, DRDO, CSIR, and other agencies :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

Vision

To nurture competent scientists, engineers, technologists, entrepreneurs, and researchers through quality education.

Mission

  • Deliver excellence in science, engineering, management, and research through innovative teaching and dedicated faculty.
  • Continuously upgrade infrastructure and academic resources.
  • Foster faculty welfare and lifelong learning.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Community
