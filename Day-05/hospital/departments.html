Our Medical Departments

Specialized care for all your health needs

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for patients with cardiovascular diseases, offering state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic services.

Our Services

Cardiac Diagnostics

Advanced diagnostic procedures including ECG, echocardiography, stress tests, and cardiac CT/MRI.

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures like angioplasty, stenting, and valve repairs.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Comprehensive programs to help patients recover and improve cardiovascular health.

Neurology

The Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Our Services

Neurological Diagnostics

Comprehensive evaluations including EEG, EMG, nerve conduction studies, and advanced imaging.

Stroke Care

24/7 stroke intervention with our dedicated stroke team and thrombolytic therapy.

Neurorehabilitation

Specialized rehabilitation programs for patients with neurological conditions.

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics Department provides comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, from sports injuries to joint replacements and spinal disorders.

Our Services

Joint Replacement

Advanced hip, knee, and shoulder replacement surgeries using minimally invasive techniques.

Sports Medicine

Specialized care for sports-related injuries and performance optimization.

Spinal Care

Comprehensive treatment for spinal disorders including disc herniation and scoliosis.

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics Department provides compassionate, family-centered care for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment.

Our Services

Well-Child Care

Regular check-ups, immunizations, and developmental screenings.

Pediatric Specialties

Specialized care for asthma, allergies, diabetes, and other childhood conditions.

Child Life Services

Programs to help children cope with hospitalization and medical procedures.
