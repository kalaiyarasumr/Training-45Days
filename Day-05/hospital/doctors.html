Dr. Anita Sharma

Cardiologist
MD, Cardiology
15+ Years Experience

Dr. Anita Sharma is a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in treating complex cardiovascular conditions. Dr. Ravi Patel

Neurologist
MD, Neurology
12+ Years Experience
English, Hindi, Gujarati

Dr. Ravi Patel is a renowned neurologist specializing in stroke care and movement disorders. After completing his residency at Mayo Clinic, he joined our team to establish the Comprehensive Stroke Center. Dr. Patel is actively involved in clinical research and has published numerous papers on innovative stroke treatments. His patient-centered approach ensures personalized care for each individual.
